Privacy Policy for Tab Monitor

Tab Monitor does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

All data (such as tab titles, URLs, or timestamps) is processed locally within the user's browser and never leaves the user's device. No information is sent to any server, and the extension does not use cookies, analytics, or any third-party tracking technologies.

This extension respects your privacy 100%.
